COSMIC CAFE

Cosmic cafe is a virtual reality game that immerses players in the role of running their own coffee shop in an intergalactic setting.

Players will use real-life coffee making techniques to brew and serve a variety of coffees to a diverse cast of robotic customers. The main goal of the game is to successfully run the coffee shop, keeping the customers happy and satisfied

Bullet Blast

Bullet Blast takes place on a fast-moving conveyor belt, where glass panels of different colors are constantly scrolling towards the player.

The users use their colored pistols to destroy and dodge glass obstacles. They can test their reflexes and aim as they try to reach the highest level possible.
